Climate Crisis Impact Dashboard - India

Climate-Related Deaths

Annual mortality due to climate events

Heatwaves, floods, and climate-induced diseases are the leading causes of climate-related deaths. The trend shows a consistent increase in mortality, reflecting the growing intensity and frequency of extreme climate events.

Economic Impact on GDP

Percentage decline and costs in billions USD

India's GDP has been adversely affected by climate events, with a visible decline over the years. Economic losses, measured in billions of USD, are driven by damages to infrastructure, healthcare costs, and disaster recovery efforts.

Agricultural Crisis

Yield reduction and crop losses

Climate change has significantly reduced agricultural yields due to droughts, unseasonal rainfall, and soil degradation. The yield index has declined steadily, while the percentage of crop losses continues to rise, threatening food security.

Employment Crisis

Sector-wise job displacement in millions

Job displacement due to climate change is most pronounced in the agricultural sector, followed by industrial and service sectors. Losses are attributed to extreme weather events disrupting production and supply chains.

Recovery Rates of India

Improved resilience and recovery over the years

Despite the damages, India's recovery rates have improved over time due to enhanced resilience, better infrastructure, and disaster management strategies. The trend suggests growing capability to bounce back from climate-related challenges.

Sectors Affected by Climate Change

Distribution of impact across different sectors

The impact of climate change varies across different sectors, with agriculture, infrastructure, and public health being the most affected. Understanding this distribution helps in prioritizing adaptation and mitigation strategies.
